About

Who are we?

Crating Specialties is a full-service, custom crating company located in Kent, Washington.

We've been around since 2004—and we've never been about anything but crates. In the last 10 years, we've grown from 2 employees to 28; gone from independent to corporate-owned, and back again; and moved up the road from Fife to our current facilities in Kent, Wash.

What makes us different?

Our customer service sets us apart. With 10 years of experience in the crating industry, we know and understand what customers are looking for. That's why we custom build every single crate we sell. We pride ourselves on service, as well as our great products.

We go on-site to take measurements for every order—in fact, we prefer to do it that way—and delivery from Crating Specialties is always free.
